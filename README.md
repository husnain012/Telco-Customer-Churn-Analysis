# Telco Customer Churn Analysis (Power BI)

## Overview
This project analyzes customer churn behavior in a telecom company to identify key drivers of customer attrition and provide actionable insights for retention.

## Problem Statement
Customer churn directly impacts revenue. The goal of this analysis is to understand:
- Which customers are more likely to churn
- What factors influence churn behavior
- How businesses can reduce churn risk

## Dataset
- 7043 customer records
- Features include demographics, services, contract type, and billing information

## Tools & Techniques
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
- Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
- DAX (Measures & Calculations)

## Dashboard Highlights
- Executive overview with key KPIs
- Customer segmentation by contract, services, and demographics
- Churn risk analysis using tenure and monthly charges
- Service usage impact (Tech Support, Total Services)
- Interactive tooltips for detailed insights

## Key Insights
- Month-to-month contracts show the highest churn rate
- Customers with low tenure are at highest risk of leaving
- Higher monthly charges increase churn probability
- Customers without tech support are more likely to churn
- Customers with fewer subscribed services show higher churn behavior

## Business Impact
This analysis helps identify high-risk customer segments and supports data-driven strategies to improve customer retention and reduce churn.
